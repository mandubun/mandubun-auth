Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Mandubun is a Chrome extension that helps you learn Korean by reading Naver Webtoons. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. Data We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address — collected when you sign in with Google via Supabase authentication. Used to identify your account and sync your progress across devices.

Usage Data

  • Learning progress — vocabulary words practiced, XP earned, streak data, and episode completion. Stored locally and synced to our backend so your progress persists.
  • Webtoon metadata — series and episode identifiers from comic.naver.com pages you visit while using the extension. Used to provide translations for the content you are reading.
  • Device timezone — your timezone (e.g., "America/New_York") is sent with requests to correctly calculate daily practice streaks and day boundaries. This is not used for location tracking.
  • Analytics events — we track your interactions with the extension to understand how features are used and to improve the learning experience. This includes: learning sessions started and completed, practice mode activity (starts, completions, and abandoned sessions), vocabulary answers (word, correctness, and response time), vocabulary lookups, onboarding steps, and chapter access. These events are tied to your account and stored on our backend.

Error Reports

  • Crash and error data — we use Sentry to collect error reports including stack traces, browser version, and extension version. This helps us fix bugs. No personal content or browsing history is included in error reports.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide translations and vocabulary features on Naver Webtoon pages
  • Save and sync your learning progress
  • Process payments for premium features (via Stripe)
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Analyze feature usage to improve the learning experience

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the extension:

  • Supabase — authentication and database hosting
  • Google Cloud Vision — optical character recognition (OCR) to extract text from webtoon images, processed on our backend
  • OpenAI — translation and language analysis, processed on our backend
  • Stripe — payment processing for premium subscriptions
  • Sentry — error monitoring and crash reporting

Webtoon image data sent for OCR and translation is processed in real time and not stored by these services beyond what is necessary to fulfill the request.

4. Data Storage

  • Locally — authentication tokens, preferences, and cached progress are stored in Chrome's local storage on your device.
  • Server-side — your account, learning progress, and vocabulary data are stored on our backend (api.mandubun.com) hosted securely.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above as necessary to operate the extension.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at support@mandubun.com. Upon account deletion, all server-side data will be removed. Local data can be cleared by uninstalling the extension.

7. Permissions

The extension requests the following browser permissions:

  • comic.naver.com — to inject the translation overlay on webtoon pages
  • image-comic.pstatic.net — to access webtoon panel images for OCR
  • api.mandubun.com — to communicate with our backend API
  • storage — to save your preferences and auth tokens locally
  • tabs — to open login and payment pages in new tabs

8. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@mandubun.com.
